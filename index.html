2D Game and Concept Artist, with 8+ years of combined experience in game art, graphic design, and product design. Artium Magister graduate of the National Academy of Fine Arts and Architecture, with a strong foundation in classical composition, color, drawing, and illustration.

Alongside production work, I have taken on leadership responsibilities, including onboarding designers, training teams of 10+, coordinating tasks during a Team Lead’s absence, and supporting smooth project transitions and workflow improvements.
